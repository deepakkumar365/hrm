About Individual Allocations
  • Individual allocations allow you to override the default allocation for a specific employee
  • These are used for exceptions where an employee's leave allocation differs from their designation or group
  • For example: An employee might get additional leave days for being a senior member or for other reasons
  • Individual allocations take precedence over designation and employee group allocations
  • Use the override reason field to document why an employee has a custom allocation
