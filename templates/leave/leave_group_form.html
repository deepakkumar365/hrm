About Leave Groups
  • Leave Groups allow you to categorize employees and assign different leave entitlements to each group
  • Each group can have its own allocation for different leave types (Annual, Sick, Casual, etc.)
  • When you assign a leave type to a group, all employees in that group automatically get those leave days
  • Groups are company-specific - each company manages its own leave groups
  • You can change an employee's group anytime in their profile
