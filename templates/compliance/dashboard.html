Singapore HR Compliance Playbook
Reference essentials for payroll, tax, and work pass adherence
CPF Contributions
  • Submit monthly contributions by the 14th of the following month.
  • Employee contribution: typically 20% (age-based tiers apply).
  • Employer contribution: typically 17% (age-based tiers apply).
  • Monthly ordinary wage ceiling currently capped at S$6,000.
AIS (Auto Inclusion Scheme)
  • Annual filing deadline on 31 March for IRAS submission.
  • Mandatory for employers with ≥ 5 staff or S$2,200/month salaries.
  • Ensure complete income records and allowances are captured.
  • Electronic transmission via IRAS portal is required.
MOM OED Declaration
  • File monthly by the 15th for all foreign employees.
  • Include salary, occupation, and work permit particulars.
  • Track pass expiry dates to avoid non-compliance.
  • Late filing risks fines or suspension from MOM.
Operational Safeguards
  • Maintain auditable employee records for 5 years.
  • Sync HR and payroll data sources weekly.
  • Retain proof of submissions and payment receipts.
  • Schedule quarterly internal compliance reviews.
