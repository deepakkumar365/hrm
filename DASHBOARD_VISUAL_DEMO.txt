                        6. OTHER DETAIL VIEWS

🟣 PURPLE "LEAVES" Card → Shows:
   - Leave requests with status (Approved, Pending, Rejected)
   - Filter by: Status, Department, Leave Type
   - See: Employee, leave type, dates, days, reason
   - Action: Approve or reject leave requests

🔵 BLUE "PAYROLL" Card → Shows:
   - Monthly payroll details
   - Filter by: Month, Year, Department
   - See: Employee, salary breakdown (basic, allowances, deductions, net)
   - Action: Verify and review payroll

🔷 CYAN "OT" Card → Shows:
   - Overtime requests with approval status
   - Filter by: Status, Department
   - See: Employee, OT date, hours, reason, status
   - Action: Approve or reject OT requests

🟠 ORANGE "EMPLOYEES" Card → Shows:
   - Employee directory with search
   - Filter by: Status, Department, Search by name/ID
   - See: Employee details (ID, name, email, dept, designation, join date)
   - Action: View employee information, update records
